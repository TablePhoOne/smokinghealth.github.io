Some not so fun facts about vaping

Vaping worse than smoking?

according to a study done by truth initiative, JUUL, a well known vape product can actually contain as much nicotine as 20 cigarettes in 1 JUUL pod.

What are you inhaling?

Vapes can actually contain the same of the same chemicals found in cigarette smoke, such as, chromium, formaldehyde, lead, and nickel.


Like having working heart? Quit Vape.

Vaping is shown to raise your blood pressure and spikes your adrenaline, which increases your heart rate and the likelihood of having a heart attack in the future.

Vapes safe for lungs? think again

“People need to understand that e-cigarettes are potentially dangerous to your health,” says researcher Blaha. “Emerging data suggests links to chronic lung disease and asthma, as well as associations between dual use of e-cigarettes and smoking with cardiovascular disease. You’re exposing yourself to all kinds of chemicals that we don’t yet understand and that are probably not safe.”

I can quit whenever I want
